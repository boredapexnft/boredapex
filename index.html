Staking

Each ‘Captain’ receives a return of $5 BOREDAPEX per day for 1 year. Captains are NFTs from the original collection of our first 1,500 NFTs. All NFTs from the original collection will have the rank of captain in the terrain NFT that will be created. Captains can claim their $BOREDAPEX daily and use it to buy, sell or trade in-game assets or they can choose to sell it to other owners or visitors on DEXs.

Additionally, NFT owners can stake their NFTs in 3, 6 or 12 month increments. 20% of land revenue will be allocated to stakers. 4% of the mutual funds will go to those who have wagered for 3 months, 6% will go to those who have wagered for 6 months and 10% will go to those who have wagered for 12 months.
